Rice University International Genetically Engineered Machine (iGEM) 2019

Our 2019 iGEM project focused on increasing drought resistance in Arabidopsis thaliana by engineering rhizobacteria (specifically Pseudomonas putida). To make translation of rhizobacterial enzymes thermodependent, we required custom-designed low temperature RNA thermometers. I wrote a Python program (and accompanying GUI) which implemented a genetic algorithm utilizing Distributed Evolutionary Algorithms in Python (DEAP) and NuPack to produce low temperature RNA thermometers.
