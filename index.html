About Me

I'm a rising junior at Rice University looking for full-stack development, data science, and systems engineering internships. I enjoy working in fast-paced team environments with a continuous learning mindset and rigorous team feedback. I aim to use interdisciplinary thinking to create innovative tools which benefit the social good and solve outstanding problems.

In April 2020, I became a mobile and web developer at Minimap, a location-based social media network that aims to connect people based on events of mutual interest. I'm also doing research analyzing data on wearable devices as part of the PATHS-UP (Precise Advanced Technologies and Health Systems for Underserved Populations) REU (research experience for undergraduates) at Rice University.

Between April 2019 and April 2020, I was the treasurer and a team lead at riceapps, an application development organization at Rice University that builds software for community partners. You can find our work and contact us at riceapps.org.

In my free time, I enjoy dancing (both k-pop and hip-hop choreographies!), reading, and experimenting with cool tech. I'm the team manager for BASYK, a dance club at Rice University. You can find our YouTube channel here.
